# HELL'S ESCAPE
A game where you play as a knight who is cursed by demon king to be enternally trapped in hell and even death can not free you from it ,
now you are trying to escape from hell and end the curse ,
to do so you have to fight all way from hell to Midgard [EARTH] 


## THEME 
endless >> You ask why , see below

the game features a 4 level presets and once you cross a roomyou are teleported to any of the 4 rooms
but yeah like not literal copy when u start clearing rooms , you will face more and more monseters in a room
so if you don't die you can continue endlessly ,  or even if u die u get telepoted into spawn room where u can spent coins to increase your abilities


# CORE MECHANISMS 
It features a endless loop of rooms that you need to clear , but as you progress furthur more enemies spawn and you can't keep up 
but there are some treasure rooms where you can collect gold and use that gold after you die at ability altar in spawn room to upgrade yourself
